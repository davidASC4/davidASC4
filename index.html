About

HTML, Cascading Style Sheets, and JavaScript

In terms of programming skills, I have used JavaScript, HTML, Cascading Style Sheets, Ruby, Arduino, and have worked with various APIs.

My short term goals include finishing high school and going to a college where I can major in something I love. Some of my long term programming goals include learning and mastering Java, as in today's programming scene, it is one of the most sought skill sets by tech companies.
